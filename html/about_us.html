About Unbeaten Inc.


Created in 1990 by a small group of enthusiasts.

Unbeaten Inc. is a privately owned retail business centred in Vancouver, British Columbia. We have grown over the years from a small one shop store selling egg-beaters to a multi-retail sector selling any variety of beaters, mixers, blenders, etc. here in Canada. Our primary focus is driving quality and ensuring the longevity of our products and thereby ensuring customer satisfaction.
